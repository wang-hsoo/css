ZOO
Zoo is a Paris-based graphic design studio founded in 2013 by Baptiste de l’Espinay, Julie Galand and Valentine Thébaut. We provide creative and art direction, digital, print and editorial design in the fields of art, cultural institutions and brands. We believe in strength of plain ideas and in necessity of a clear language. Each project is an adventure with new issues, driven by the care of functionality and aesthetic. Feel free to contact us, it will be a pleasure to meet you.
